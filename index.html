ABOUT ME

I am currently a student at the University of Birmingham Coding Bootcamp. As part of the course, I have been working on individual projects as well team projects alongside my fellow students. I have gone from creating HTML web pages to building full stack web applications with front and backend. Some projects I have worked on Code-quiz, Password-Generator, Bored-as-Book, Note-Taker, Weather-dashboard-display and Unified are to mention a few. To find out more about all the projects I have worked on please visit my GitHub profile.
