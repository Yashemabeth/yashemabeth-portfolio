ABOUT ME

Yashema continues to work with Stingray Records and her latest singles “Zion” released 5th March 2021 and “Conversation” which is due to be released on 7th may 2021 were produced by Carlton ‘Dillie’ McLeod. Her Most recent works saw the release of her Debut Album “Awakening” Produced by Carlton ‘Dillie’ McLeod on 13th November 2020. Tracks like “I’m Just a Girl”, “Ooh La La’’ and “Prophecy” are featured on the Album. Her songs are a combination of Reggae, Lovers, Roots, Spiritual and Inspirational.
